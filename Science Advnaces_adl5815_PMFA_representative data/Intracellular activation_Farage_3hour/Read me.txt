Meaning of abbreviations and notations:

- Sol : adding soluble poteins of interest with same amount of proteins coated on beads
- MB : Magnetic Bead coated proteins
- MB+ : In the presence of a magnet lid (placing a magnet lid to apply magnetic force to magnetic beads)
- MB - : In the absence of a magnet lid
- TPA :  tetradecanoylphorbol acetate
- AM : anysomicin
